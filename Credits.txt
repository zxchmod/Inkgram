Inkgram Credits
===============

Inkgram is a free and open-source modification of Telegram (GPLv3 licensed).

This project includes contributions and integrations from the following:

- Telegram (https://github.com/DrKLO/Telegram) – the original open-source Telegram client for Android.
- Apertium (https://www.apertium.org/) – free and open-source machine translation platform used for message translation.
